Low% Speedrun of Twilight Princess

Low% speedrunning is beating a game with the fewest items possible. An accidentally-discovered exploit in The Legend of Zelda: Twilight Princess allows a particularly interesting route.

When Link picks up an item, he shows it to the camera and a looped animation plays. This loop is missing its last frame. If it isn't stopped by the player, Link noclips backwards ever so slightly each time it plays.

So, trigger this animation when standing with your back to locked doors to get through... after 17 hours of waiting. Still a speedrun because the rules are least items wins!
